ACR Poker Rake and Rakeback

Limit, No-Limit, and Pot-Limit Games.

In ACR Poker, rake is taken from each pot, with the commission size depending on the number of players participating in the hand. In tournaments, the rake size is 10%, while in Spins, it's 6%.

The ACR Poker loyalty program offers players the chance to earn back part of their rake through six military ranks: Lieutenant, Captain, Major, Colonel, General, and Army General. For every dollar of rake generated at any table, players receive 5.5 Rank Points (RP). The exchange rate of RP to Combat Points (CP) depends on the player's rank, with higher ranks offering better exchange rates. CP can be exchanged for cash in the Rewards section of the client at a rate of 100 CP = $1. The more you play, the more RP you accumulate, leading to more CP, higher ranks, and better exchange rates.

Beginners and casual players should know that the loyalty program at ACR Poker follows a military-style ranking system with six ranks, starting from Lieutenant and ending with Army General. For every $1 of rake generated at any table, players earn 5.5 Rank Points (RP). The exchange rate of RP to CP (Combat Points) depends on the rank you hold. As you progress through the ranks, the exchange rate becomes more favorable. You can easily convert Combat Points into cash in the Rewards menu of the client at a rate of 100 CP = $1.

However, it's important to remember that to maintain the last two ranks (General and Army General), you need to earn at least 7,000 and 45,000 Rank Points respectively each month (except January) to retain your status for the year.
